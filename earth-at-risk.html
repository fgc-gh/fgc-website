Earth At Risk

From 2010 to 2014, Fertile Ground Conservancy organized the Earth At Risk conference series in the San Francisco Bay Area — a platform for biocentric, anti-capitalist ecological thought that brought together leaders from Indigenous, feminist, anti-war, anti-racist, and environmental movements.

Over 1,000 people attended across the series. Speakers included Arundhati Roy, Vandana Shiva, Alice Walker, Chris Hedges, Derrick Jensen, Lierre Keith, and Dominique Christina, among many others. The full video archive is available below.
