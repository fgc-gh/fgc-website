Support the work.

Fertile Ground Conservancy is a small organization doing work that larger institutions won't. Your donation funds land protection, the organizational infrastructure that makes fiscal sponsorship possible, and the ongoing work of building a conservation movement rooted in biocentric values.

We are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible to the extent permitted by law. EIN: 27-1194001.

Other ways to give
  • By check — make payable to Fertile Ground Conservancy and mail to: Fertile Ground Conservancy, PO Box 11262, Eugene, OR 97440
  • Stock or securities — contact us at [email protected] to arrange a transfer
  • Planned giving / bequest — include Fertile Ground Conservancy in your will or estate plan. Contact us to discuss options
  • Land donation — if you own land you would like to protect, we'd like to talk
Where it goes
  • Land stewardship — property taxes, legal fees, and long-term holding costs for the Elk Creek parcel and future acquisitions
  • Fiscal sponsorship infrastructure — accounting, compliance, grant management, and organizational support for sponsored initiatives
  • Operations — insurance, filing fees, communications, and the basic costs of keeping a nonprofit functional
  • Future land acquisition — building toward additional parcels in the Elk Creek watershed and elsewhere in Cascadia
